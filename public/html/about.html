Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic gave us a glimpse into the future of digital smart cities. Lockdowns and isolation made it imperative for everyone to operate virtually- be it school, business, or leisure. As the pandemic prevailed, we also got a window to assess the problems that existed in our cities. Delhi, for one, faced one of the harshest situations. Shortage of hospital beds, unavailability of vaccines, misinformation, scarcity of water, inequitable distribution of resources, and inefficient public transport and water supply being some of many. Desperate cries for medical supplies and help were seen all over social media for months- which points to a much bigger and scarier aspect of not only our healthcare system, but our city's management altogether. Digital aid, namely social media, was arguably the most advantageous service in these times of crisis. This leads us to believe that the sudden prevalence of digital media, which further points to smart cities, may have come about due to necessity, but smart cities are definitely our answer to the many ubiquitous challenges that will continue to plague our cities- pandemic or not.

Solutions

“The Future is arriving more quickly and technology is providing the means with which to shape it”

Cordexer Systems provides a very simple solution to the problems facing our city- a uniform software management system with an extensive database to cater to the needs of each and every citizen. It is simple, comprehensive and easy-to-use, making sure that its benefits are ready to be exploited by everyone- not just someone who is a tech-wiz.

Features

  1. Interactive User Interface

    2. Cordexer provides a simple and attractive user interface- making sure each experience at the website is an easy and memorable one.

  2. Real-time Updates on Vaccination Slot Availabilities

    3. Cordexer provides extensive data on the availability of slots for vaccination in the city, which is updated in real time. It is quick and efficient, and without any lagging.

  3. Real-time tracking of Public Transport and Water Supply

    4. The real-time location and routes of public transport such as buses, metros, etc. can be obtained through Cordexer by RFID chips embedded in the vehicles. Water transport vehicles can be tracked in a similar manner to ensure timely delivery.

  4. Personalised Infographic and Schedule to Combat Diabetes

    5. A study conducted by the Associated Chambers of Commerce and Industry of India reported that Delhi has the highest number of diabetes patients in the country at 42.5% of the total population of Delhi. Cordexer provides a comprehensive infographic on diabetes that people can access at any time. It also provides a feature for people to schedule their meals, exercise and medicines to promote a healthier lifestyle and combat diabetes.

  5. Hassle-free Portal to Streamline Communication between the Government and the Governed

    6. Inefficient communication lines between the government and the citizens are one of the root causes of failed administrations. Cordexer provides a simple and effective way to streamline communication. It provides a portal for people to fill out their grievances or queries, which are sorted and filtered through an AI system and redirected to the concerned authorities. This saves time and effort, and is much more prompt and convenient for all parties involved.

  6. Special Page to Feature Local Businesses and Artisans

    7. COVID has been a hard time for all businesses, but businesses who did not have digital reach or the means to access virtual marketing were especially hard hit. Cordexer provides a special interactive feature for these businesses to promote their livelihood in an economically feasible and practical manner through blogs.
